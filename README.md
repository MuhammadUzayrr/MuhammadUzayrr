## About Me

I'm a Computer Science graduate from The Shaikh Ayaz University (SAUS), Shikarpur, Pakistan. I build things at the intersection of AI, mobile development, and social impact — from singer booking apps to carbon trackers to fake skill detection systems.

I care about making AI useful for real problems, especially in contexts like Pakistan where the technology often isn't built with us in mind.

Right now I'm:
- Co-founding the **Digital Youth Leadership Programme (DYLP)**
- Working on the **SAUS university portal** as part of the TSAUS developer team
- Preparing for graduate school in AI/ML
